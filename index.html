About BCC

Our History & Mission

Binalbagan Catholic College is a private Catholic educational institution located in Binalbagan, Negros Occidental. We are committed to providing holistic education that integrates academic excellence, moral values, and spiritual formation.

Mission: To form competent, compassionate, and committed individuals rooted in Christian values, serving God and country.

Vision: A leading Catholic institution transforming lives through quality education and faith-based service.

Our Programs

Basic Education

Kindergarten, Elementary, Junior High School, Senior High School (STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS)

College Department

Bachelor of Elementary Education, Bachelor of Secondary Education, BS in Business Administration, BS in Information Technology, BS in Criminology

Graduate School

Master of Arts in Education, Master in Business Administration
